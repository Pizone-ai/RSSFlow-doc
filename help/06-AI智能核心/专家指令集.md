# 专家指令集 / Expert Command Set

### 中文

专家指令集是 RSSFlow Pro 中用于指导 AI 产出特定专业视角分析报告的提示词模版。通过一键选用这些精心调优的专家指令，您可以直接让 AI 以专业的行业研究员、宏观经济分析师、战略咨询官等视角对您的 RSS 资讯进行过滤和深度解构。

**内置专家级指令一览：**
系统预装了 22 个涵盖不同专业领域的深度分析角色：
1. **创意构思者 (Ideator)**：发现并勾勒当前内容中可能成为热门的话题集群。
2. **趋势筛选器 (Trend Selector)**：为您甄选并评估最具传播潜力的话题趋势。
3. **草稿构建器 (Draft Builder)**：将选中的新闻素材快速梳理为一篇逻辑清晰的文章大纲或草稿。
4. **人性化改写 (Humanizer)**：优化文本语气，消除生硬感，使文章表达更加流畅自然。
5. **风险雷达 (Risk Radar)**：自动评估当前产业地缘政治、合规性及外部潜在风险信号。
6. **行业雷达 (Sector Radar)**：分析目标行业的供给、需求动态与竞争态势。
7. **宏观视图 (Macro View)**：解读全球宏观经济、流动性与行业基本面。
8. **外汇量化 (Forex Quant)**：评估利率走势与宏观经济基本面情绪。
9. **战略官视角 (CSO Board)**：从商业战略角度分析企业的竞争壁垒与核心价值捕获能力。
10. **加密情报 (Crypto Intel)**：追踪区块链、DeFi 与加密数字资产的重要动向。
11. **事实简报 (Fact Brief)**：将不同来源的复杂冲突陈述梳理成客观、交叉验证的事实报告。
12. **动态分析 (Dynamics)**：为多变量系统与行业连锁反应建立关联逻辑。
13. **推特长文 (Threader)**：将深度研究内容精炼，生成适合社交网络（如 X/Twitter）分享的互动内容。
14. **市场简报 (Market Brief)**：通过视觉化陈述映射市场逻辑流。
15. **科技日报 (Tech Daily)**：深剖科技企业平台、资本开支壁垒与商业化成果。
16. **去噪分析 (De-noiser)**：透过第一性原理，快速过滤冗余信息，直击核心不变量。
17. **技术博主 (Tech Onboarding)**：从工程技术角度评估新工具的接入成本和演进路径。
18. **思维教练 (Mind Coach)**：引导低成本尝试，提供诊断和决策突破建议。
19. **多米诺模拟 (Domino Sim)**：模拟并追溯供应链异常对各个层级产生的潜在多米诺骨牌影响。
20. **林迪过滤 (Lindy Filter)**：剥离短期时效性噪音，帮您过滤出最具长远价值的经典概念。
21. **Serenity 方法 (Serenity Method)**：审计商业系统中的原材料清单和核心瓶颈。
22. **贝叶斯交易 (Bayesian Trade)**：分析特定关键事件的概率影响，从而制定理性的对冲决策策略。

*(注：部分高级专业级指令需订阅并激活 Pro 版本后解锁)*

**自定义个人专属指令：**
- **灵活拓展**：除了内置的 22 个专家指令，您可以在设置页面添加、修改或删除您专属的自定义指令，并可设定定制的分析模板。
- **混合显示**：您的自定义指令会与系统预设的指令完美合并，直接显示在聊天输入框的快捷指令菜单中，供您一键调用。

**使用方式：**
- 在 AI 聊天对话框中，点击命令图标打开指令菜单，点击选中目标指令，AI 即可立刻代入该专家角色对当前的所有文章进行深入研判。

### English

The Expert Command Set contains pre-configured analysis profiles in RSSFlow Pro. By selecting a command, you direct the AI to analyze your gathered RSS feeds from specialized perspectives—such as a macro economist, industry strategist, risk auditor, or trend researcher.

**Built-in Expert Commands:**
The system includes 22 pre-tuned role profiles:
1. **Ideator**: Discovers and maps out viral topic clusters from feed data.
2. **Trend Selector**: Evaluates and isolates high-potential social media and industry trends.
3. **Draft Builder**: Assembles aggregated RSS materials into a structured draft article.
4. **Humanizer**: Polishes machine-like copy, enhancing readability and flow.
5. **Risk Radar**: Audits geopolitical risks and compliance signals.
6. **Sector Radar**: Identifies supply-demand dynamics and competitive matrices in target sectors.
7. **Macro View**: Breaks down central bank policies and global market liquidity.
8. **Forex Quant**: Assesses yield differentials and macroeconomic sentiments.
9. **CSO Board**: Evaluates company moat, value capture, and business dynamics.
10. **Crypto Intel**: Tracks decentralized finance (DeFi), tokenomics, and web3 indicators.
11. **Fact Brief**: Cross-references conflicting reports into verified briefs.
12. **Dynamics**: Models feedback loops across multiple market variables.
13. **Threader**: Condenses dense research into highly engaging X/Twitter threads.
14. **Market Brief**: Maps price barriers and logical pathways visually.
15. **Tech Daily**: Deconstructs double-sided platforms, capital expenditure, and tech moats.
16. **De-noiser**: Filters noise to extract core facts using first principles.
17. **Tech Onboarding**: Reviews development costs and technical debt of new developer tools.
18. **Mind Coach**: Targets execution paralysis and guides low-friction micro-experiments.
19. **Domino Sim**: Models supply-chain disruptions caused by black-swan anomalies.
20. **Lindy Filter**: Filters temporal noise, isolate invariant concepts.
21. **Serenity Method**: Evaluates BOM dependencies and technological bottlenecks.
22. **Bayesian Trade**: Approximates probabilities of key events to construct hedging plans.

*(Note: Advanced professional commands require Pro version activation)*

**Custom Commands:**
- **Add Personal Prompts**: You can easily create, edit, or delete your own custom prompts in the settings dashboard.
- **Unified Palette**: Custom prompts merge seamlessly with built-in commands and show up under the chat commands menu for one-click deployment.

**Usage:**
- In the AI Chat input box, open the commands menu and select any command. The AI will instantly process your loaded articles through that specific analysis filter.
