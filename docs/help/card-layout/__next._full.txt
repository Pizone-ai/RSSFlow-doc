<ol>
<li><strong>Header</strong>: feed name · AI indicator · relative time · favorite (on hover)</li>

<h2>Multi-column Grid</h2>

<ul>
<li>~≥ 680px: 2 columns</li>
<li>~≥ 1020px: 3 columns</li>
</ul>
