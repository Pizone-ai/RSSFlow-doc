# 订阅源管理与分类

## 已订阅源列表

在选项页面的"订阅源"（选项页中的“订阅源”标签页）标签页中，"已订阅源"区域显示所有已添加的 RSS 订阅源，每个条目包含：

- **标题**：订阅源的名称（`feedTitle`）
- **URL**：订阅源的 RSS 地址（`feedUrl`）
- **错误指示器**：
  - 失败次数 > 2 时显示警告图标（`warning_amber`）
  - 失败次数 ≥ 5 时显示错误图标（`error_outline`，带脉冲动画）
  - 鼠标悬停显示提示（`feeds.errorTooltip`）：失败次数和最后尝试时间
- **Auto AI 开关**：切换该订阅源的自动 AI 摘要功能。开启前会检查 AI 配置是否有效，若未配置则提示前往 AI 设置页面
- **删除按钮**：点击后弹出确认对话框，删除该订阅源及所有历史文章（后台指令）

## 分类

RSSFlow Pro 目前没有手动分类管理界面。分类通过以下两种方式实现：

1. **推荐订阅源分类**：在"推荐订阅源"区域按中文/英文分类展示，但不影响订阅源本身的数据结构
2. **AI 标签分类**：在 AI 设置中配置的标签范围（`tagEnumerationValues`）用于 AI 自动为文章添加标签，标签分类定义在  的 `TAG_CATEGORIES` 中

## 自动 AI 摘要

开启自动摘要后，新文章将自动触发生成 AI 摘要。如果想要发挥Rssflow的最大能力，需要开启这个开关。

这个是rssflow 高级AI功能的一个基础，如果不提前做摘要，后面直接对文章全量或者RAG的片段进行高级分析，这个上下文会爆掉，后续再压缩也是会有很多信息丢失，尽可能提前对每篇文章进行多维度的要点分解，需要的时候再取出来，在质量和信息完整性更优，在后续的高级分析和推演上会更出色的结果，这个是当前全文或者RAG知识库层面也很难解决的。

# Feed Management & Categories

## Subscribed Feeds List

In the Feeds tab of the settings page, the "Subscribed Feeds" section lists all added RSS feeds. Each entry shows:

- **Title**: Feed name
- **URL**: RSS address
- **Error Indicator**:
  - Warning icon when failure count > 2
  - Error icon when failure count ≥ 5
  - Hovering displays tooltips showing the failure details and last attempt time
- **Auto AI Toggle**: Enables/disables automatic AI summary for this feed. It checks AI configuration validity first and prompts you to visit the AI Settings page if not configured
- **Delete Button**: Shows a confirmation dialog, deletes the feed and all historical articles

## Categories

RSSFlow Pro currently does not have a manual category management interface. Categorization works through two mechanisms:

1. **Recommended Feed Categories**: Displayed in the Recommended Feeds section by Chinese/English categories, which does not affect feed data structure
2. **AI Tag Classification**: Tag scopes configured in AI Settings are used by AI to automatically tag articles. Tag categories are defined inside the configuration settings.

## Auto AI Summary

When enabled, new articles automatically trigger AI summary generation. To maximize the power of RSSFlow, this toggle should be enabled.

This serves as the foundation for RSSFlow's advanced AI capabilities. If summaries are not generated upfront, sending full articles or arbitrary RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) chunks to the model for heavy analysis later will easily overflow the context window, and subsequent compression will lead to massive information loss. Decomposing every article into multi-dimensional key points upfront, and then retrieving them when needed, yields superior quality and completeness. This approach achieves far better results in downstream advanced analysis and reasoning—a challenge that current full-text databases and standard RAG knowledge bases fail to address.
