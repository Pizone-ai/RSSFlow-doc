# 主题与外观 / Theme

### 中文

RSSFlow Pro 拥有极佳的主题渲染表现，全面适配浅色与深色视觉偏好，能有效缓解夜间阅读疲劳，保护您的视力。

**主题切换：**
- **操作方式**：在侧边栏底部的工具栏中，点击“主题切换”图标（太阳/月亮图标），即可在以下三种模式间循环切换：
  - ☀️ **浅色模式**：适合光线充足的白天阅读，文字清晰锐利。
  - 🌙 **深色模式**：专为夜间或暗光环境优化，降低屏幕亮度刺激。
  - 💻 **跟随系统**：自动检测您电脑或手机操作系统的深色偏好，实现无缝同步切换。

**全面覆盖的视觉系统：**
主题切换后，RSSFlow 的所有功能板块均会自动实时变色，包括：
- 侧边栏列表及主视图
- 常规设置与 AI 配置等选项页面
- 沉浸式阅读器（Zen Reader）页面
- 智能 AI 对话与图谱星系面板

**设置持久化：**
- 您的主题选择会自动安全保存在您的浏览器本地，即使关闭浏览器或电脑重启，重新打开时依然会完美保留您喜欢的外观模式。

---

### English

RSSFlow Pro features custom-tailored theme rendering for both Light and Dark modes, reducing eye strain during extended reading sessions.

**Theme Selection:**
- **How to Switch**: Click the theme icon (Sun/Moon) in the bottom toolbar of the sidebar to cycle through three modes:
  - ☀️ **Light Mode**: Perfect for well-lit environments, featuring high contrast and sharp typography.
  - 🌙 **Dark Mode**: Optimized for night reading or low-light conditions, minimizing screen glare.
  - 💻 **System Default**: Automatically syncs with your operating system's color preferences.

**Consistent Design Language:**
Changing the theme instantly adapts all views and panels, including:
- Sidebar feeds and main content streams
- Settings, options, and dashboard pages
- Zen Reader page
- AI Chat panels and Graph/Discovery views

**Persistence:**
- Your theme preference is saved locally in your browser. It automatically persists across sessions so you never have to re-configure it.
