# 三种报告发布模式 / Report Publish Modes

### 中文

当自动化任务运行完毕并生成深度分析简报后，RSSFlow Pro 支持将生成的精美报告以网页形式发布出去，供您直接阅读或分享给他人。您可以在任务列表上方的**“发布设置”**中进行全局配置。

**1. 默认发布通道（官方托管）：**
- **介绍**：直接使用 RSSFlow 官方托管的安全发布通道。
- **优点**：零配置，开箱即用。生成的文章页面布局极其精美，非常适合在手机上快捷浏览与传阅。

**2. 自定义发布通道（自建托管）：**
- **介绍**：如果您拥有自己的网页托管账号，希望自己托管所有的简报数据，可选择此通道。
- **配置**：需要填写您自建的网页托管地址与安全密钥。
- **优点**：数据完全归您自己所有，且可以自由设置报告的保存天数（如保留最近 180 天的简报，过期自动清除）。

**3. SEO 博客门户发布（独立站点）：**
- **介绍**：如果您运营着自己的个人博客或者资讯小站，可以将生成的精选报告直接自动推送到您的独立站点中。
- **配置**：需要配置您的站点网址和安全密钥。
- **优点**：支持自定义文章分类栏目、专栏系列以及作者名称，实现全自动的精彩内容呈现。
- **真实运行范例**：您可以访问官方的示例阅读小站：[blog.oinchain.com](https://blog.oinchain.com)。该站点是一个**完全由 RSSFlow Pro 全自动整理和更新**的个人精选阅读空间。通过访问该网页，您可以直观地感受到由 RSSFlow 整理出的高质量文章内容、清晰舒适的排版布局，以及全自动定期更新的轻松体验。

**报告的标签合并逻辑：**
为了确保生成的报告分类准确且利于检索，系统在发布报告时会自动合并以下三种标签：
1. **全局预置标签**：您在 AI 设置中预先设定的全局主要分类标签。
2. **任务专属标签**：您在此定时任务中设置的文章筛选标签。
3. **发布默认标签**：您在发布配置中设定的此报告默认附加标签。

### English

Once a workflow finishes compiling insights, RSSFlow Pro supports publishing the formatted reports online so you can read, archive, or share them. You can configure these channels in the **"Publish Settings"** modal.

**1. Default Channel (Official Hosting):**
- **Overview**: Uses RSSFlow's official hosting infrastructure.
- **Benefits**: Zero-configuration, works out of the box. Generates a clean, readable mobile-friendly web page for instant reading and sharing.

**2. Custom Channel (Self-Hosted):**
- **Overview**: If you prefer to have absolute ownership over your data, you can self-host the publishing service on your own web infrastructure.
- **Setup**: Requires your self-hosted API URL and security access keys.
- **Benefits**: Complete privacy and control. You can customize the retention period of published reports (e.g. automatically delete reports older than 180 days).

**3. Blog Portal Integration (SEO Sites):**
- **Overview**: Automatically publishes compiled reports directly into your personal blog or custom reading space.
- **Setup**: Requires your website URL and security token.
- **Benefits**: Supports mapping articles to specific categories, series folders, and author names.
- **Live Example**: You can visit our official demo reading space at [blog.oinchain.com](https://blog.oinchain.com). This entire website is a **personally curated reading site fully managed and updated automatically by RSSFlow Pro**. By exploring this site, you can directly experience the readable insights, polished structure, and clean layout of the curated articles, showing how easily you can maintain your own digital reading space.

**Tag Merging Logic:**
To ensure published reports are correctly cataloged and easy to search, the system automatically merges tags from three sources upon publication:
1. **Global Classification Tags**: General domain tags defined in your main AI configuration.
2. **Task Filter Tags**: Specific tags chosen to filter inputs for this particular workflow.
3. **Default Publication Tags**: Default tag metadata appended to all reports published through this configuration.
