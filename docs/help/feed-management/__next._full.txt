1b:T4bb,<h2>Access</h2>
<p><strong>Settings → Feeds → Subscribed Feeds</strong></p>
<h2>List Fields</h2>
<p>Each feed typically shows:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Title</strong> and <strong>URL</strong></li>
<li><strong>Error indicators</strong> after repeated update failures (hover for details)</li>
<li><strong>Auto AI</strong> toggle for per-feed automatic summaries (requires AI setup)</li>
<li><strong>Delete</strong> with confirmation (removes feed + historical articles)</li>
</ul>
<h2>Categories</h2>
<p>There is no separate manual folder UI. Organization usually comes from:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Recommended feed categories</strong> (discovery only)</li>
<li><strong>AI tags</strong> configured under AI Settings, applied during summarization and used by Flow / Graph / workflows</li>
</ol>
<h2>Why Enable Auto Summary</h2>
<p>Auto summary is the foundation for advanced AI:</p>
<ul>
<li>Decompose each article into points, tags, and scores up front</li>
<li>Skipping it forces large full-text contexts later and loses quality after compression</li>
<li>Workflows, discovery, and cited chat work best on structured articles</li>
</ul>
