# 双击标记已读与木鱼音效 / Double-click Dismiss & Wooden Bell

### 中文

双击操作是 RSSFlow Pro 中用于快速整理阅读列表的高效交互方式，配合轻快的功德木鱼音效，让信息整理变得轻松且解压。

**双击标记已读：**
- **操作方式**：在侧边栏文章列表中，**双击**任意文章卡片。
- **视觉反馈**：双击后，文章卡片会触发一段 150 毫秒的折叠收缩动画，随后从当前未读列表中移除。该设计能防止单击和双击冲突，不影响正常单击打开阅读器。

**木鱼音效：**
- **功能介绍**：当您双击将文章标记为已读时，系统会播放一声轻快的“木鱼”敲击音效，象征着信息清理的“功德+1”。
- **音效设置**：您可以根据喜好自由开启或关闭此音效。
  - **路径**：选项页面 → 通用设置 → “启用双击已读音效”开关。
  - **默认状态**：默认开启，音量预设为 40%，既清晰又不刺耳。

**新手指引：**
- 首次使用扩展且列表内有未读文章时，系统会自动弹出引导弹窗提示此快捷操作，点击“知道了”后不再自动弹出。

### English

Double-clicking is a quick and satisfying way to organize your reading list in RSSFlow Pro. Paired with a calming "wooden bell" sound effect, cleaning up your inbox becomes a stress-free habit.

**Double-click to Dismiss:**
- **How to use**: Simply **double-click** any article card in the sidebar.
- **Visual Feedback**: The card instantly triggers a 150ms collapse animation and is removed from your unread list. This ensures double-clicks never conflict with single-clicks (which open the reader).

**Wooden Bell Sound Effect:**
- **How it works**: Marking an article as read plays a brief, pleasant "wooden bell" sound, celebrating your productivity.
- **Sound Settings**: You can toggle this sound effect at any time.
  - **Path**: Options → General Settings → "Enable double-click sound" toggle.
  - **Volume**: Pre-configured at a comfortable 40% volume level.

**Onboarding Hint:**
- On your first use with unread articles in the list, an interactive hint pops up to guide you through this shortcut. Dismissing it saves your preferences and won't auto-show again.
